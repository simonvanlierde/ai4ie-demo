A curated reading list with three kinds of entries: **AI applied in IE** (tagged by
IE field and AI technique), **research practice** (reproducibility, verification,
disclosure), and the **ML landmarks** behind today's tools. Every entry says in one
line why it's worth your time.

Filter by any combination of tags; filtered views are shareable URLs. Entries live in [`src/data/literature.yaml`](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/src/data/literature.yaml); adding one is a small pull request, see the [contributing guide](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
