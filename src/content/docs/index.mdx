A community-curated guide to using AI in industrial ecology (IE). It started in the
**AI4IE working group** — IE researchers scattered across institutes, who meet on
[Discord](https://discord.gg/zhHAZakXHX) — and is open to contributions from anyone.

:::note[A proposal: argue with it]
This website is currently in the demo phase. If something looks wrong or your subfield is missing, [open a pull request](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
:::

## How it fits together

One tag vocabulary connects the whole site. For every application area you can
follow the same three steps:

1. **See what works today** on the [map of AI applications](/applications-map/), e.g. computer vision at end-of-life;
2. **Read the evidence** in the [literature list](/literature/), pre-filtered to the same tags, e.g. [EoL × computer vision](/literature/?ie=eol&ml=cv);
3. **Try it** on the [demos page](/demos/) where a demo exists, e.g. zero-shot recognition running in your browser.

## Sections

| # | Section | What's in it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | [Map of AI applications in IE](/applications-map/) | What works today, by IE field × AI technique, with a maturity call for each entry. |
| 2 | [Literature & resources](/literature/) | A tag-filterable reading list: AI in IE, research practice, and ML landmarks. |
| 3 | [Best practices & guides](/best-practices/) | Getting started, pitfalls, verification checklists, reusable prompts, Claude skills. |
| 4 | [Tools & hardware](/tools/) | Models, apps, libraries, and what to run them on, including local options for sensitive data. |
| 5 | [Demos](/demos/) | In-browser and embedded demos, each tied to a cell of the map. |

## Contributing

Add a paper, a tool, an application, or a demo by opening a pull request; data
lives in two YAML files and every page has an edit pencil. The
[contributing guide](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
explains the format and where each type of entry goes.
