A map of where AI is being applied across industrial ecology, organized along **two
dimensions**: the IE field (LCA, MFA, waste & circularity, …) and the AI technique
(LLMs, computer vision, tabular ML, …). Every entry links to the
[literature list](../literature/) pre-filtered to the same tags, so the map doubles as
an index into the reading list.

:::note[Seeded proposal]
Entries are seeded by working-group members and honestly imperfect. Argue with them,
and add what's missing: entries live in
[`src/data/applications.yaml`](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/src/data/applications.yaml);
see [Contributing](https://github.com/simonvanlierde/ai4ie-demo/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
:::
