One member's **draft working taxonomy** of computer-vision tasks for product-scale
data collection at end-of-life: a worked example of how one corner of the
[applications map](/applications-map/) can be mapped in depth. Treat it as a starting
point, not settled work.

## How this map works

For each taxonomy category:

- **What it covers:** one or two lines, in plain terms.
- **Examples:** real applications, tools, or papers (link them; don't fabricate).
- **Demo:** an optional embedded web-app demonstrating the category.

## Taxonomy at a glance

Two axes: **physical scale** (product, component, material) and **information type**
(identity, quantity, structure, condition). Each populated cell is a CV task carrying a
maturity verdict. Read against its own rubric, the map is largely negative: no cell reaches
*strong* under end-of-life capture, and the useful signal sits in the gradations below it.

| Scale ↓ / Info type → | Identity | Quantity | Structure (BoC) | Condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Product** | Label OCR + retrieval (**P**) | Depth / multi-view geometry (**P**) | — | Wear / damage grading (**U**) |
| **Component** | Marking OCR + open-vocab naming (**P**) | Per-part geometry, occluded (**P**) | Detection (**P**) / attachment relations (**E**) | Connector-state grading (**U**) |
| **Material** | Surface BoM classification (**P**); full BoM an open gap | Derived (geometry × density) | — | Corrosion / contamination (**U**) |

**Maturity:** **P** partial · **E** emerging-but-narrow · **U** plausible-but-unvalidated ·
— not directly sensed (empty or derived). No cell is *strong* under decentralized EoL
capture.

_A personal working taxonomy, still in draft, scoped to CV for product-scale data
collection. Verdicts and coverage will shift as it develops._

## Product-level tasks

**What it covers:** data recoverable from an image of the whole product.

**Examples:**

- **Identity (partial).** OCR metadata extraction from labels (text detection, recognition,
  then fuzzy match to a reference database), with retrieval-based classification and VLM
  scene-text reading as complements. Deployed precedent: a washing-machine label pipeline
  run by non-experts on commodity phones read model numbers from 95.7% of label-visible
  images in professional repair but only 39.7% at a recycler <Cite id="sterkensProductLabelIdentification2023" />.
  Failure mode: label degradation at EoL, plus a database-coverage limit downstream.
- **Quantity (partial, approximate).** Monocular metric depth (<Cite id="huMetric3DV22024">Metric3D v2</Cite>, <Cite id="bochkovskiiDepthProSharp2024">Depth Pro</Cite>,
  <Cite id="yangDepthAnythingV22024">Depth Anything v2</Cite>), feed-forward multi-view geometry (<Cite id="wangVGGTVisualGeometry2025">VGGT</Cite>, <Cite id="keethaMapAnythingUniversalFeedForward2025">MapAnything</Cite>), and
  photogrammetry / <Cite id="mildenhallNeRFRepresentingScenes2020">NeRF</Cite> / <Cite id="kerbl3DGaussianSplatting2023">3D Gaussian splatting</Cite> recover error-bounded geometry; single-image
  mass estimation exists but is coarse. Visible surfaces only; reflective, transparent, and
  thin parts are the limiting cases.
- **Condition (plausible but unvalidated).** Wear, damage, and corrosion recognition feeding
  repair / reuse / recycle triage. Nearest method is zero-shot anomaly detection (<Cite id="jeongWinCLIPZeroFewShot2023">WinCLIP</Cite>,
  <Cite id="zhouAnomalyCLIPObjectAgnostic2023">AnomalyCLIP</Cite>), which flags an anomaly's presence, not a graded per-unit condition, and is
  unvalidated on worn consumer products.

**Demo:** the [zero-shot identification demo](/demos/#in-this-browser-zero-shot-identification-clip)
runs the open-vocabulary part of this cell in your browser.

## Component-level tasks

**What it covers:** data about the individual components inside an opened product.

**Examples:**

- **Structure / BoC (partial detection, emerging-but-narrow relations).** The most populated
  cell. Detecting, segmenting, and counting components is well-validated where task-tuned
  (~98.8% mAP50 on a laptop-teardown benchmark); foundation segmenters (<Cite id="kirillovSegmentAnything2023">SAM</Cite>, <Cite id="raviSAM2Segment2024">SAM 2</Cite>, <Cite id="carionSAM3Segment2025">SAM 3</Cite>,
  <Cite id="liuGroundingDINOMarrying2024">GroundingDINO</Cite>, <Cite id="renGroundedSAMAssembling2024">Grounded-SAM</Cite>) need an added class-assignment layer. Inferring
  component-to-component *attachment relations* is the weakest cell: single-image methods
  (<Cite id="liuSINGAPOSingleImage2025">SINGAPO</Cite>, <Cite id="chenURDFormerPipelineConstructing2024">URDFormer</Cite>) exist only for a few articulated household categories, collapse out
  of distribution (~82% → ~39%), and recover kinematic joints rather than the fastener
  relations (screwed, glued, clipped) a disassembly BoC needs.
- **Identity (partial).** OCR on component markings plus one-shot / retrieval recognition and
  open-vocabulary naming from a text prompt; fine-grained brand- or part-specific identity is
  not reliably recovered.
- **Quantity (partial, approximate).** Inherits the product-level geometry methods,
  aggravated by occlusion within the assembled product.
- **Condition (plausible but unvalidated).** Connector-state recognition (screws, clips,
  solder joints) for disassembly planning; published work shows fastener *detection*, not
  per-unit condition *grading*.

## Material-level tasks

**What it covers:** the material composition (bill of materials) of the product and its
parts.

**Examples:**

- **Identity / BoM (partial on visible surfaces, open gap for full BoM).** Surface material
  classification and segmentation of *visible* regions (<Cite id="bellMaterialRecognitionWild2015">MINC</Cite>'s 23-class in-the-wild baseline;
  one/few-shot similarity given a region mask; <Cite id="radfordLearningTransferableVisual2021">CLIP</Cite>-based recognition, fragile under domain
  shift and multi-material compositeness). One deployed setting delivers image-based
  composition: an industrial sensor sorter on shredded WEEE (<Cite id="redmonYouOnlyLook2015">YOLO</Cite>, mAP50 ≈ 0.99). But it
  characterizes an aggregate flow at a central plant, not the per-component composition of an
  intact product. Occluded interior composition is an open gap.
- **Quantity (derived, not sensed).** Per-material mass or volume would come from component
  geometry × material class × a density prior; no dedicated method, and it compounds both
  inputs' uncertainty. Forward-looking.
- **Condition (plausible but unvalidated).** Corrosion, degradation, and contamination;
  surface segmentation can isolate regions, but per-region grading on arbitrary products is
  not validated.
